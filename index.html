About Marisa

Marisa Marion Mackenzie (she/her/hers) is an openly aro ace, gay and queer individual who is both a trans woman and genderqueer non-binary at the same time.

She has called Nogojiwanong-Peterborough, Ontario, Canada her home for many years.

She has lived and living experiences of being abused, being neglected, being oppressed and silenced, trauma, marginalization, poverty, homelessness, and a myriad of mental and physical health challenges.

She is an activist and advocate. She enjoys helping and bringing hope, joy, and happiness to others.

In her spare time, she enjoys art, graphic design, reading, writing, learning, computers and technology, walking, listening to music, and spending time with those close to her.

About This Website

www: I decided to force/redirect all web traffic to this www portion because people often associate www with a website, and not everyone is aware '.gg' is a valid top-level domain much like '.com' or '.net', for example.

1r1s: My name is often misspelled, mispronounced, miscapitalized, and often all done deliberately by those in power and authority out of spite, malice, incompetence, and ignorance. This part signifies and codifies that my name is spelled with 1 'r' and 1 's'.

gg: I had decided to go with '.gg' because I was worried while planning my website '1r1s' would be considered a 'premium' domain and I didn't want to pay an absurd amount of money for those. '.gg' doesn't have 'premium' domains, but it turns out '1r1s' wasn't considered 'premium' by my domain registrar. I decided to stick with '.gg' anyway. I lovingly joke that it's short for 'good girl'. *gets head pats*

I wanted to have a lil' slice o' web to call my own, so I made a website. This is the website's second incarnation, reborn like a Phoenix, rising from the ashes.

Thank you for visiting www.1r1s.gg
